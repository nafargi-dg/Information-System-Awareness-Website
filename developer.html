Academic Odyssey

Greetings! I am Nafargi damena, a passionate Information Science student at Addis Ababa University, where I am delving into the exciting intersection of technology and information. Beyond my academic journey, I bring over six years of enriching experience as a Graphics Designer, Web Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Application Developer for both platforms.

Currently pursuing my studies in Information Science, I find immense joy in exploring the ever-evolving landscape of data, technology, and problem-solving. My academic pursuits are driven by a curiosity to understand the profound impact of information on our world and how technology can harness its potential.

Graphics Design and Web Development

My journey in the professional realm began with a passion for visually compelling design and functional digital experiences. Over the years, I've honed my skills in graphics design and web development. I believe in the power of aesthetics and seamless user experiences. From crafting eye-catching graphics to developing responsive websites, I have embraced the challenges and joys of this creative journey.

UI/UX Design

As a seasoned UI/UX designer, I understand the importance of creating interfaces that not only captivate but also enhance user interactions. My designs are rooted in user-centric principles, ensuring that every click and swipe is an intuitive and enjoyable experience.
